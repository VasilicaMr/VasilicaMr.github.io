DebatableAI SaaS • Under Development

Medical AI Review & Knowledge Platform

DebatableAI is a SaaS tool where medical professionals review, debate, and approve AI-generated answers to common health questions. Approved content can then be published in a clinic’s knowledge section (via API) to help patients find reliable guidance.

Product

A web + mobile platform that helps medical teams collaborate on AI responses and produce approved, publishable knowledge entries.

  • Clinician discussion threads around an AI response
  • Approval workflow (draft → approved/published)
  • Organization/clinic knowledge feed and search endpoint
  • Audit trail and role-based access (doctor/moderator/admin)

Who it’s for

Clinics and medical organizations that want internal oversight of AI-assisted answers and a controlled way to publish vetted content.

  • Clinics
  • Medical groups
  • Private practices
  • Healthcare content teams

Payments

Users subscribe monthly per organization to access premium features, including AI-assisted discussions, approval workflows, and the clinic knowledge API/feed.

Important information

DebatableAI is intended to support clinicians and clinic teams. It is not a substitute for professional medical judgment. Public-facing content is published only after clinician review/approval (where enabled). For emergencies, patients should contact local emergency services.

Privacy: The platform is designed to minimize sensitive data; organizations are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable laws and internal policies.
